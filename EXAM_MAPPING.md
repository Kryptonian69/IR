# IR Practical Exam Mapping Guide

## 1. Inverted Index & Retrieval
- **Keywords:** "Inverted Index", "Retrieve documents containing terms"
- **Files:** `Practical1_a.py` (Construction), `Practical1_b.py` (Retrieval)
- **Action:** Update strings `doc1`, `doc2`, etc., and the `query` variable.

## 2. Boolean & Vector Space Model
- **Keywords:** "Boolean retrieval", "AND/OR/NOT", "TF-IDF", "Cosine Similarity"
- **Files:** `Practical2_a.py` (Boolean), `Practical2_b.py` (TF-IDF/Cosine)
- **Action:** Update the `corpus` list or `documents` list.

## 3. Edit Distance (Spelling)
- **Keywords:** "Levenshtein distance", "Edit distance", "Spelling correction"
- **Files:** `Practical3_a.py`, `Practical3_b.py`
- **Action:** Update `input_word` and the `dictionary` list.

## 4. Evaluation Metrics
- **Keywords:** "Precision", "Recall", "F-measure", "Average Precision"
- **Files:** `Practical4_a.py` (Manual sets), `Practical4_b.py` (Toolkit)
- **Action:** Update the sets of doc IDs or the `y_true`/`y_scores` lists.

## 5. Classification
- **Keywords:** "Naive Bayes", "Classification", "Predict class"
- **Files:** `Practical5_a.py`, `Practical5_b.py`
- **Action:** Update `X_train`, `y_train`, and `X_test` with slip data.

## 6. Clustering
- **Keywords:** "K-means", "Silhouette Score", "Group similar documents"
- **Files:** `Practical6_a.py`, `Practical6_b.py`
- **Action:** Update the `documents` list and set `k` (n_clusters).

## 7. Web Crawling
- **Keywords:** "Web crawler", "robots.txt", "Crawling delays"
- **Files:** `Practical7_a.py` (Basic), `Practical7_b.py` (Advanced)
- **Action:** Update the target `URL` and `max_pages`.

## 8. PageRank
- **Keywords:** "PageRank algorithm", "Web graph", "Rank pages"
- **Files:** `Practical8_a.py`, `Practical8_b.py`
- **Action:** Update the `graph` or `web_graph` dictionary with the slip's links.

## 9. Learning to Rank
- **Keywords:** "Learning to rank", "RankSVM", "RankBoost"
- **Files:** `Practical9_a.py`, `Practical9_b.py`
- **Action:** Usually requires no changes unless specific data is provided.

## 10. Summarization & QA
- **Keywords:** "Text summarization", "Extractive", "Question-answering system"
- **Files:** `Practical10_a.py` (Summary), `Practical10_b.py` (QA)
- **Action:** Update `sample_text`, `context_text`, and `question`.
